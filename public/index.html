Industrial Solutions with Rotorise

Parts Provision in Diverse Fields

We are a trustworthy, professional, and well-experienced provider of parts and equipment for rotary machines.

We combine technology, experience and an abiding commitment to teamwork, to meet the needs of our clients in the oil, gas, petrochemical and energy industries.

  • Through our office in Austria we have no restriction in supply of high-quality materials and equipment from worldwide manufacturers.
  • Our business philosophy is based on close cooperation with customers and partners, finding solutions for cost effective and reliable operation of your equipment. Independent and international organizational structure enables us to respond swiftly and flexible.
  • With our competitive labor rates, quality, efficient working methods, and parts sourcing ability, we offer you a significant reduction in costs in comparison to OEM rates.
